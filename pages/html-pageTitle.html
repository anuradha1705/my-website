HTML Page Title

The Page Title is the text that appears on the browser tab when you open a webpage. It's defined inside the <title> tag in the <head> section.

Syntax

<head>
  <title>This is my page title</title>
</head>

Why is Page Title Important?

  • It appears on the browser tab.
  • It helps with Search Engine Optimization (SEO).
  • It improves the usability and accessibility of the website.

Example

In this example, the page title is set to "Welcome to DevByAnu":

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
  <title>Welcome to DevByAnu</title>
</head>
<body>
  <h1>Hello World!</h1>
</body>
</html>
