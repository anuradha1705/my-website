Embedding YouTube Videos in HTML

You can easily embed YouTube videos in your webpage using the <iframe> tag. This allows visitors to watch videos directly on your site without leaving it.

<iframe width="560" height="315"
  src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID"
  title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0"
  allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture"
  allowfullscreen>
</iframe>

üìù Explanation

  • The <iframe> tag is used to embed another HTML page or video inside your page.
  • The src attribute points to the YouTube video URL in embed format.
  • Adjust the width and height attributes to control the player size.
  • allowfullscreen enables fullscreen mode for the video.
  • The allow attribute specifies permissions like autoplay and clipboard access.
